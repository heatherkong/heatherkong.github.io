Rating: Title: Description:
1-HarryPotter and The Deathly Hallows-After the death of everyone that has ever cared about him, Harry is devastated. Voldemort learns about the deathly hallows, and wants to use the Elder Wand to kill Harry. Harry finds out he must die to make Voldemort more vulnerable...

2-HarryPotter and The Order Of Pheonix-Harry's Godfather and the rest of the mysterious Order of The Pheonix bring Harry, Ron, and Hermione to Sirius's house. The Dark Lord is after a weapon, and would stop at nothing to get it...

3-HarryPotter and The Prisoner of Azkaban-There is a murderer, guilty of handing Harry's parents over to voldemort, and break-ins at hogwarts means that Hogwarts itself is not safe. When Harry gets attacked by dementors, he hears his parents screaming and he wants to get revenge...

4-HarryPotter and the Half Blood Prince-In Harry's 6th year, Harry learns that Draco Malfoy is a Death Eater. Hogwarts itself is not safe. Dumbledore is weak, cursed somehow, and Harry follows instructions from a mysterious potions book that someone had written instructions on. Harry must break up with Ginerva Weasley for her safety...

5-HarryPotter and the Chamber of Secrets-In Harry's 2nd year, mudbloods are petrified by a mysterious creature, including Harry's best friend, Hermione. He and Ron need to stop the attacks, only to find out that Ginerva Weasley is in trouble...

6-HarryPotter and the Philospher/sorcerer's stone-Harry's birthday passes, and he finds himself face-to-face with a gentle giant of a man, Hagrid. He makes friends with Ronald Weasley, and Hermione Granger. They find that there is a 3headed dog guarding a treasure putten there by the kind headmaster Albus Percival Wulfraic Brian Dumbledore...

7-HarryPotter and the Goblet Of Fire-Harry is entered in the triwizard by some mysterious person, and is hopelessly in love with Cho Chang, fellow seeker. He needs to fight every foul thing in the universe, and Voldemort murders a fellow champion, Cedric Diggory of hufflepuff...

8-The Tales Of Beedle the Bard-These tales are fun to read about, and the most famous one is the percival brothers. The 3 brothers meet death, and death gives them a gift. The eldest asks for the elder wand-one that is unbeatable, and can be taken only by murder, The 2nd brother wants the reserection stone, to see his dead girlfriend once more, THe 3rd and cleverest brother asked for an invisibility cloak, so that he would be safe from death. The eldest was killed, the 2nd joined his girlfriend, and the 3rd died a natural death, passing on the cloak to his son...

9-Fantastic beasts and where to find them-Newt Scamender wrote all about beasts and where they can be found...

10-Quidditch through the Ages-Harry Potter depended on this book in Philospher stone. This book teaches the basics of quidditch and is really fun to read..
